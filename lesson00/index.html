SAY All-Purpose Phrases

Each phrase plays twice: slow → natural speed.

Sumimasen
Excuse me. / I'm sorry.
Arigatoo gozaimasu
Thank you.
Moo ichido onegaishimasu
One more time, please.
Yukkuri onegaishimasu
Slowly, please.
Eego de onegaishimasu
In English, please.
Kore wa nihongo de nan desu ka?
How do you say this in Japanese?
Toire wa doko desu ka?
Where is the toilet?
Daijoobu desu
I'm fine. / No thank you.
