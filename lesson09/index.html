DIALOGUE Manga Dialogue
Rina
Sumimasen. Kasa o wasuremashita.
Excuse me. I forgot my umbrella.
Staff
Donna kasa desu ka?
What does it look like?
Rina
Kuro desu. Chiisai desu.
It's black. It's small.
Staff
Itsu desu ka?
When was this?
Rina
Kyoo desu.
Today.
Staff
Shooshoo omachi kudasai.
Please wait a moment.
Rina
Arigatoo gozaimasu!
Thank you!

PRACTICE Listen & Respond

Question → 3.5-second pause (say your answer!) → model answer.
