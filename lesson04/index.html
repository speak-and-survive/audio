SAY Phrases to Say

Each phrase plays twice: slow → natural speed.

Kinkakuji ni ikitai desu. Deguchi wa doko desu ka?
I want to go to Kinkakuji. Where is the exit?
Kitaguchi wa doko desu ka?
Where is the north exit?
Sumimasen, wakarimasen
…Sorry, I don’t understand…
HEAR Phrases You'll Hear
Asoko desu.
Over there.
Kochira e doozo.
This way, please
